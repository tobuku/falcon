Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Data Collection

This site is hosted as a static site on GitHub Pages. No server-side form submissions are stored. Form data is copied to clipboard for manual processing.

Analytics may be added in the future. If added, this policy will be updated to reflect what is collected.

Listing Data

Listing data includes public contact details submitted by business owners or found in public sources. All listing data is stored in static JSON files within the site repository.

Cookies

This site does not set cookies. If third-party analytics are added in the future, cookie usage will be disclosed here.

Removal Requests

To request removal of your business information from the directory, submit a request through the contact page. Requests are processed within 5 business days.
