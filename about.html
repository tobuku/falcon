About Falconry Directory USA

Everything falconry. One place. We connect falconers with schools, instructors, breeders, abatement providers, vets, and gear sellers across the United States.

How Listings Work

  • Free listings show basic business info, category, and contact details.
  • Verified listings display a badge after phone, website, and credential proof checks.
  • Featured listings receive top placement in category and state search results.
  • Report flows allow anyone to flag bad data or outdated information.

Verification Rules

  • Phone and website are checked for active status.
  • Permit claims are stored as text and are state-specific.
  • Credentials fields store permit level, years in practice, and public proof links.
  • Verification applies to paid tiers only.

What We Cover

  • Falconry schools, apprentice programs, and instructor matching.
  • Raptor breeders and mentorship sponsors.
  • Pest bird abatement providers for commercial and agricultural use.
  • Raptor veterinarians and wildlife rehabilitators.
  • Gear sellers: gloves, hoods, jesses, telemetry, mews builds, and more.
  • Falconry organizations, clubs, and events.

Future Plans

  • Add country selector and international listings.
  • Add language fields per listing.
  • Add region-level filters below state.
  • Add international permit notes per jurisdiction.
  • Payments integration for verified and featured tiers.
